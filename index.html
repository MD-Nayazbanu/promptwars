AI Planning Logic

StudyGenius AI doesn't just divide days by subjects. It acts like a real tutor, making strategic decisions based on constraints:

  1. Dynamic Priority Scoring: Each subject receives a score computed as: (Urgency × 2) + Weakness + Time Constraint. This ensures high-yield topics get attention when time is scarce.
  2. Risk Confidence: The AI evaluates feasibility and assigns a Risk Level (High/Medium/Low) based on available hours vs workload, so you know exactly where you stand.
  3. Edge-Case Intelligence: If you input too many subjects for too few hours, the AI automatically drops low-priority topics or compresses the plan to ensure a realistic passing score.
  4. Simulation Engine: Clicking "Skip Today?" recalculates your entire schedule with one less day, instantly showing the consequences of procrastination (higher risks and heavier daily workload).
  5. Revision Mode: If your exam is 3 days or fewer away, the AI aborts learning new material and switches entirely to "Revision Mode" for retention.
  6. Smart Optimization: Clicking "Optimize Plan" allows the AI to re-evaluate and aggressively trim standard topics to secure your weak points.
